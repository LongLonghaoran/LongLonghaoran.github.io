导出review数据
笔记本：Anker

需要导出PN A3951（A3951011，A3951021，A3951031，A3951051）产品在德国和英国亚马逊的review评论需求，用于展示到soundcore美国官网上面，谢谢哈～
reviews = Review.where(
  sku: %w(A3909011), marketplace: %w(amazon amazon_uk amazon_uk2 amazon_de amazon_de2)
).where("created_at > ?", DateTime.new(2021,1,1)).
select(
  *%w(id amazon_review_id sku asin marketplace review_created_date nick_name orginal_star orginal_message current_star current_message)
)
CSV.open(Rails.root.join("review_infos.csv"), "wb") do |csv|
  reviews.each do |review|
    csv << [
      review.id, review.amazon_review_id, review.sku, review.asin, review.marketplace, 
      review.review_created_date, review.nick_name, review.orginal_star, review.orginal_message,
      review.current_star, review.current_message
    ]
  end
end
